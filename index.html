About

Built by operators for founders making high-stakes leadership decisions.

Asteria was created after seeing talented founders lose quarters to poor-fit search partners. Our mission is to make executive search firm selection transparent, rigorous, and founder-centered.

We have sat inside hiring rooms where urgency, investor pressure, and limited market knowledge collided. Asteria turns that experience into a calm, evidence-backed selection process.

Mission

Help ambitious companies select search partners based on fit, not visibility.
